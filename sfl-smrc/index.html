About SFL-SMRC

SFL-SMRC is a Regional Innovation Cluster funded by the SBA to help small and mid-sized manufacturers across South Florida become more competitive through digital tools, ERP systems, AI-powered analytics, and capital access support. We work with advanced manufacturing firms in Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties, providing comprehensive support to drive innovation, efficiency, and growth in the manufacturing sector.

Our Mission

Accelerate digital transformation and resilience in South Florida's manufacturing ecosystem.

Our Vision

Support South Florida businesses in smart and advanced manufacturing, driving innovation, growth, and stronger supply chains.

Our Approach

Collaborative partnerships with industry leaders, academic institutions, and government agencies.
